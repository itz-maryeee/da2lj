Freddy Sifumac

//PAPUA NEW GUINEA

Freddy Sifumac, from Morobe province of Papua New Guinea, was in Cebu, Philippines before the pandemic to study Political Science at the University of Southern Philippines Foundation. After joining a local church in 2019, he was then discipled by DA2LJ Philippines’ President, Mr. Jimmy Colmenares. The 10-lesson one-on-one discipleship strengthened his faith and built his genuine friendship with Jesus.

With Conversations, I learned new insights in my faith walk with Jesus Christ. The journey allowed me to experience and build genuine friendship with Jesus. I am excited to swim deep in this material, in His Word, and disciple others, especially my friends and families from Papua New Guinea.

Batak Tribes

//PAPUA NEW GUINEA

Though we’ve launched Conversations early 2020, many of our partner churches and individuals are still using our old discipleship workbook and have proven it effective in their respective ministries.

Mark Joseph Ibale, our Assistant Coordinator from Palawan, shared that the ministry has been growing in the community of the Batak Tribes of Palawan using the old discipleship workbook. Just recently, DA2LJ welcomed 11 more Qualified Disciplers from Batak Tribe with most of them in their teenage years.

Their story is a good reminder for all of us that no matter our age, we can take part in the Great Commission. And we can do this!

Marilyn Besinga

//GENERAL SANTOS CITY

Marilyn, 26, was at the Mactan-Cebu International Airport when the Philippine government announced an immediate lockdown in all local and international flights due to the rising cases of Coronavirus Disease or COVID-19 in the country. Marie got stranded in Cebu and chose to stay with a family friend turned discipler in the first few months of the pandemic. It was at this time that Marie and her discipler, Ms. Shane Pearl Palcis started their one-on-one discipleship using Conversations.

I felt freedom. During the process, the deepest and darkest sins I was keeping for the longest time were exposed. But I experienced turning to God in genuine repentance. I felt His forgiveness. I felt the love and acceptance that I was longing for — which no one could ever suffice but Jesus.
